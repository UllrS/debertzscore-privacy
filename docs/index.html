Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 11, 2026

Debertz Score respects your privacy.

This app is designed to help users record and track scores in card games, including Debertz and Tertz.

1. Information We Collect

Debertz Score does not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal data.

The app does not request your name, phone number, email address, location, payment information, or any other personal information.

2. Local Data Storage

Information related to app usage, such as:

  • player scores,
  • game history,
  • saved game records,

is stored locally on your device only and is not sent to the developer’s servers.

3. Analytics, Advertising, and Tracking

The app does not use:

  • analytics services,
  • advertising SDKs,
  • tracking technologies,
  • user accounts,
  • third-party data collection tools.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

Since the app does not collect personal data, no personal data is shared with third parties.

5. Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children or from any other users.

6. Data Deletion

Because app data is stored only on the user’s device, it can be deleted by:

  • removing saved game records inside the app, if such feature is available;
  • deleting the app from the device.

Deleting the app may also remove all locally stored data, depending on your device settings and backups.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The current version will always be published on this page with the updated effective date.

8. Limitation of Liability

Debertz Score is provided on an “as is” basis. Like any software, the app may contain bugs, experience interruptions, or result in loss of locally stored data.

You use the app at your own risk. The developer is not responsible for data loss, incorrect score calculations, app malfunctions, or any consequences resulting from the use of the app.

The app is not intended for commercial, financial, accounting, or other official record-keeping. Do not rely on it as the sole place to store important information. If your data is important, you should keep a backup elsewhere.

This app is created for entertainment purposes and is intended to make scorekeeping during games easier and more convenient.
